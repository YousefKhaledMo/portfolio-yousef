## Design Context

### Users
Dual audience: (1) Hiring managers and creative directors at agencies and companies evaluating Yousef for full-time roles, and (2) potential freelance clients looking to commission brand identity, web design, or Notion systems work. Both audiences are busy and need to quickly grasp skill + differentiation. They scan portfolios in 5-10 seconds before deciding to stay.

### Brand Personality
**Warm, thoughtful, refined** — Not cold or sterile tech-bro energy. Not flashy-for-the-sake-of-it. Warmth comes through in the prose tone and the legal-to-design story. Thoughtfulness in the structure and intentional details. Refinement in typography, spacing, and motion craft.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Cinematic storytelling with editorial precision. Awwwards-level craft — immersive, motion-driven, immersive first impressions that reward deeper exploration.
- **References**: say-hi.to, Awwwards-winning portfolio sites. Motion choreography, staggered reveals, film-like pacing.
- **Anti-references**: Generic template portfolios. Cookie-cutter layouts, predictable section patterns, lifeless grids.
- **Theme**: Dark primary (OLED black) with light mode toggle. Dark is the default identity; light is a considerate alternative.
- **Color palette**: Dark base (#0A0A0A) with red accent (#C8372E). Warm neutrals tinted toward amber/brown, not pure gray.

### Design Principles
1. **Story over sections** — The page tells a narrative (law → design), not just a list of skills. Every section earns its place in the story.
2. **Warm precision** — Every detail is intentional, but the feeling is human, not robotic. The craft is invisible until you look closer.
3. **Motion as meaning** — Animations aren't decoration. They guide attention, reveal hierarchy, and reward interaction. Film-like pacing, not jittery distractions.
4. **Restraint with presence** — Bold choices in type and color, generous whitespace, but never empty for emptiness' sake. The silences are designed too.
5. **Dual-audience clarity** — Works equally well for a recruiter scanning in 30 seconds and a creative director exploring for 5 minutes. Layered disclosure.
