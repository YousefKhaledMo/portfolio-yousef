§2 — About

A lawyer who designs

I started my career reading contracts and interpreting statutes. Today, I read users and interpret behavior. The transition from Law to Design wasn't a detour—it was the shortest path to understanding what makes people tick.

With a Bachelor of Law from Ain Shams University and certifications from Google, CalArts, and Notion, I bring a rare combination of analytical rigor and creative instinct. I don't just make things look good—I make them work.

From brand identity systems to Notion workflow architectures, from Figma prototypes to deployed websites—I manage the entire lifecycle of a digital product. Strategy, design, development, and marketing. One person, one vision.
